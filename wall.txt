Mood Music
----------

Sit down at the piano.

The camera above the keyboard watches your face and tries to read your
emotional expression — happiness, sadness, anger, fear, surprise, or
something in between. As long as it can see you, the piano plays: a
continuously improvised melody shaped in real time by whatever mood it
detects. Move away, and the music stops.

Nothing is recorded. The camera image is processed locally on the
computer and is never stored or sent anywhere.

There is nothing to press or adjust. Just sit, and listen.
